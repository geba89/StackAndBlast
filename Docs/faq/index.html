FAQ & Support

Got questions? We've got answers.

Tap a column to drop a block onto the grid. When a group of same-colored blocks reaches the blast threshold, they explode and earn you points. The bigger the group, the higher your score! Special power-up blocks appear periodically to help you clear the board.

Power-up blocks spawn every 8 pieces during gameplay. When they're included in a blast, their special effect triggers. Effects include clearing entire rows, columns, or surrounding blocks. Check the Power-Ups section in Settings for a full list.

When blocks are blasted, the remaining blocks fall and settle. If the newly settled blocks form another matching group that meets the threshold, they blast too — creating a chain reaction. Each chain in the sequence earns bonus points with an increasing multiplier.

The blast threshold is the minimum number of connected same-colored blocks needed to trigger an explosion. It starts low and increases progressively as your score grows, making the game more challenging over time.

Yes! Go to Settings and choose from 8x8, 9x9, 10x10, or 12x12 grids. The change takes effect on your next new game. Larger grids give you more room to plan, while smaller grids are more intense.

Colorblind Mode adds distinct patterns to each block color, making it easy to tell blocks apart without relying on color alone. You can enable it in Settings at any time.

When the game ends (the grid fills up), you can use a Bomb to clear a section of the board and continue playing. Watch a short ad to earn a bomb and keep your run going.

Yes, your scores, statistics, and settings are saved locally on your device. However, if you delete the app, this data will be lost. We recommend keeping regular device backups through iCloud or iTunes.

Stack & Blast is free to play, and ads help support ongoing development and new features. We keep ads non-intrusive — they only appear between games or when you choose to watch one for a reward like the Bomb.
